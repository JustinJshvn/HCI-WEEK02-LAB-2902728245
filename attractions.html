Top Attractions

Here are some well-known places to visit in and around Yogyakarta.

City Highlights

  • Tugu Yogyakarta — the city’s iconic white monument and meeting point.
  • Jalan Malioboro — a famous street with markets, food stalls, and shops.
  • Fort Vredeburg — a Dutch colonial-era fort and museum.
  • Beringharjo Market — traditional market known for batik and local snacks.

Nearby World Heritage Sites

  • Borobudur (Magelang)
  • Prambanan (near Sleman)
