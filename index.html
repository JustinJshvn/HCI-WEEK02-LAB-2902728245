Yogyakarta (Jogja)

Yogyakarta is the capital city of the Special Region of Yogyakarta, Indonesia. It is known as a center of education and Javanese culture, and it once served as Indonesia's capital during the National Revolution (1946–1948). The city is home to the Sultan's palace (the Kraton) and sits between the Indian Ocean to the south and Mount Merapi to the north.
